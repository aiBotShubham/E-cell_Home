Vision

The working of the E-Cell can directly be correlated with that of a startup. Just like any entrepreneurial venture, the E-Cell has no hierarchy, with the Secretary and the Additional Secretaries at the very top to the eager and enthusiastic volunteers from the freshmen year, the workload is quantitatively allocated in a very healthy fashion. The E-Cell not only promotes and nurtures the Entrepreneurship in the campus of IIT Guwahati, it also works diligently towards building an Entrepreneurial mindset among the Students of the Campus.
